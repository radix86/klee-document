KLEE 搜索器 (Searcher)
=========================
`Searcher` 是KLEE搜索算法的父类，在他之下还继承了许多子类，有
`DFSSearcher`
`BFSSearcher`
`RandomSearcher`
`WeightedRandomSearcher`
`RandomPathSearcher`
`MergingSearcher`
`BumpMergingSearcher`
`BatchingSearcher`
`IterativeDeepeningTimeSearcher`
`InterleavedSearcher`
